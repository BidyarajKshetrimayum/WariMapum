WariMapup
(Summary)

WariMapum' website ashi Manipuri Sahitya gi phajaraba matick magun sing ashibu seithajaduna, sejinaduna; wari (macha/lairik), seireng (mateck/mapum) na chingba kaya amabu ejaduna thagtchanaba semjabani. Malemgi tonganba mapham kaya amada khunda leitaduna leibikhraba echil enow sing amad e-mari singbu amata oiba lipuna chingsinduna/kisinduna thamnaba amadi mari sing ashibu hena afaba mari oinaba hotnajabani. Ngalingei matam amada eikhoina libry da natraga post da thiduna/thaduna wana panaba hotnarame. Adubu ngacge chowkhatpage thak asege matung ena chatnabe oina e-book ashi tangymak fadana semdaba yarakte. Mashi hy hyduna thynadage leijaraklaba chatnabe laibery da changduna lyrick paba, post ta thadok thajin twduna paba ashige chatnabe ashimasu yokhataba tangy fade. Leiramliba thynage chtnabe ashina eikhoibu socialize oihanbata natana pukning wakhal annibusu harwna momon nokna thamba ngamhali. E-book ki kanaba ama hyjaragana eina ninglakpa matamda fat changduna paba yaba, matam fangdaba kaya amabusu chtpage matam kythokauba twdana esage Mobile da yengjaba yaba ashi-sing ashi leibana kanade natraga fate hybamaksu langane. Maram sing ashi neinaduna yengbada aninimak ashi tuuna chtminadaba yadaba ama oirake. Maramshing ashi auraga semjabane.

Maruoiba maram amana, eikhoina pajaningba matamda paba fangdabage awaba ama, nwna manipuri meiteimayekta ontharakpage yamna chowba cheitheng ama thangnajei, bangalida lyrick sing ashi fanglabasu meitei mayektad fangbada khajicktang wana thokpa aujei mashigedamak khalage, hwrakleba echa enowsing ashina thirakpa matamda amadi paninglakpa matamda fanghanduna paba ngamjanabage semjabane. Amadi angamba sing d thagatlaga thamlamc. Esage ware ngakta natana B.A. Manipuri honours paper da, M.A da Manipuri tamjaningba singi khalaga websit ashida angamba makheid bangali mayek adu bangali tamhwdraba singna paba matamda mamut tana pajanabagidamakta handokcharaga thagatchagani.

Lairana hwrakpa oibana setningba chaninba ening thunglaktabasing, fiman setuna, 2 suna fwba setpa fangjaraktaba aduge mathakta epana nongmage chara tanaba hotnaba, awaba sing auba heiruba thamoicna lyrick amage mamal aubada thangei sengnamak leibiu hyningde. Hyrurabadi ngac hayeng leibirake, mashige mathakta bangali natana meiteimayekta fangdabage awaba, ojana takpada note twsinjaraga tamjarake esamksu. Esamakta thokpa thowdok ama oina lyrick leiduna tamba ngamjadaba amad website ta thagatpirakpa sing adu pdf oina downlode twjaraga bangali mayek adu emada handokhalaga tamjarake. Mashinasu loiba nate internet ta fangdaba sing aduna emanabi sing (class ta nupa etontani) makhoida leibra hangduna yraga paba. Mashida fwkiba nungytaba amadi "Asuk sona leireboire" hyna khankhiba wakha aduna liching oiraga website ashi semninglakhibani.

Esana ejaba seireng ware khak natana laioikhraba maha kabi singna ebiramba, maru oina B.A/M.A tambada ywba topic sing khangatcharaga translate twduna thagatchagane. Mashida lyrick yonbiba/fongbiriba singbu lali natraga lande, Educationge systemc soire natraga soide hyna neinafam thokte. Ahanba emitsina auba ahoba makhun adudi tungi khalaga mensinduna thamlamsi.
